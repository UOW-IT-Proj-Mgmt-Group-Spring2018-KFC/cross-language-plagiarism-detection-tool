It usually starts in the teenage years. It is estimated that about one out of every 100 adolescent girls has the disorder. 2 Anorexia nervosa (AN) refers to a kind of eating disorder characterized by intentionally lower body weight than normal by dieting and other means. It belongs to the category of "physiological disorders related to psychological factors" in the field of psychiatry. Its main characteristics are a strong fear of weight gain and obesity characterized by extreme concern for weight and body shape, blind pursuit of slim weight, significant weight loss, often malnutrition, metabolic and endocrine disorders, such as female amenorrhea. Severe malnutrition can lead to cachexia, body failure and life threatening. 5% - 15% of the patients eventually die of heart complications, multiple organ failure, secondary infection, suicide and so on.
